Featured Tools Overview

Our platform offers two advanced digital PR tools designed for modern communications professionals. The first is an AI-Powered Media Monitoring system, and the second is a Curated PR Contact & Media Database. These tools work together to automate routine tasks so your agency can focus on strategy and storytelling.

AI-Powered Media Monitoring

Our AI-powered media monitoring uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence to track and analyze brand mentions across digital channels. This tech-driven solution scans social media, news sites, blogs, and more to provide real-time insights on your campaigns.

Key Features:

  • • Real-time sentiment analysis across 50+ platforms
  • • Advanced keyword tracking with AI-powered context understanding
  • • Automated crisis detection and alert system
  • • Comprehensive analytics dashboard with exportable reports
  • • Multi-language support for global brand monitoring

Curated PR Contact & Media Database

Our Curated PR Contact & Media Database is a structured directory of journalists, editors, bloggers, and influencers. Each contact entry includes up-to-date email addresses, beat and topic interests, and outlet details to ensure precise targeting.
